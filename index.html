His role in the place value system

He also played a very major role in determining the place value system and discovering the zero. He also worked on the summation series of square roots and cube roots. He is also regarded as the first to use zero in the place value system.
He also calculated the sidereal rotation, which is the rotation of the earth in relation to the fixed stars. His theories and deductions formed the base of the trigonometry and algebra.

Tributes

Bihar has nurtured knowledge for thousand of years. The works of many scientific thinkers like Aryabhata, Panini, Varahamihira, Bhaskara, Brahamagupta and Vashist Narayan Singh can be traced to the land of Bihar.
Aryabhata (476-550 CE) was first in line of mathematician- astronomers from the classical age of Indian Mathematics and Indian Astronomy.

His major work, Aryabhatiya- a compendium of Mathematics and Astronomy was of great influence on several neighboring cultures through transitions. Aryabhata asserted that moon, planets and asterisms shine by reflected sunlight.

He also correctly explained the causes of eclipses of the sun and the moon.
